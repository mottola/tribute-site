Elon Musk

  • Innovator
  • Visionary
  • Genius
Elon is one of the greatest innovators to ever live. He has worked fervently and relentlessly to advance our species and protect our planet. Pushing the boundaries of technology and what is thought possible, transforming failures into triumphs, and persisting through doubt and ridicule, Musk has transcended the status of personal hero and has become a living legend.

Timeline

  • 1971: Elon is born in South Africa
  • 1979: At just eight years old, Elon reads ten hours a day and has read the entire Encyclopedia Brittanica.
  • 1983: At only twelve years of age, Elon codes and sells Blastar, his own video game.
  • 1985: Elon concludes that his purpose in life is to save humanity
  • 1988: Elon moves to Canada.
  • 1989: Starts college at Queen's University in Ontario
  • 1991: Transfers to the University of Pennsylvania
  • 1994: Elon graduates from the University of Pennsylvania with degrees in both Economics and Physics.
  • 1995: Musk begins his PhD program in Applied Physics at Stanford University, but soon leaves to pursue passions in industry.
  • 1995: Elon starts his first company - Zip2.
  • 1999: Zip2 sells for $22 million dollars. Musk then starts online banking company X.com.
  • 2000: Merges X.com with Confinity, thus creating Paypal
  • 2000: Elon marries his college girlfriend, Justine.
  • 2002: Musk starts SpaceX. In same year, receives $250 million for sale of Paypal.
  • 2004: Invests in an co-founds Tesla motors, the world's first all electric vehicle.
  • 2006: SpaceX wins NASA contracts to deliver cargo to the International Space Station
  • 2006: Invests in an co-founds SolarCity
  • 2008: Musk divorces Justine. Successfully launches rocket into low earth orbit after three failed attempts. Wins NASA contract for $1.6 billion dollars.
  • 2010: Elon marries Talulah
  • 2012: Elon divorces Talulah. The Dragon capsule successfully docks with the International Space Station.
  • 2013: Elon remarries Talulah. Elon also unveils plans to build Hyperloop.
  • 2015: Successfully lands rocket onto platform post orbit. A first in history.
